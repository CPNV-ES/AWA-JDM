Nissan Skyline GT-R

Nissan shook the motoring world with it's incredible new GT-R. It has genuine supercar pace (193mph, 0-60mph in 3.4sec), unbelievable handling (a 7:27 Nurburgring time), superb quality, comprehensive kit and to top it all, this one's even a looker! As in it's predecessor, technology abounds. There are systems for stability control, traction control, EBD, voice recognition sat-nav, tyre pressure, performance stats, electronic torque distribution and even a predictive shift for the dual clutch transmission.
